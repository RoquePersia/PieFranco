A little bit

of history

Born of love, our story begins in 1890, with Rosa Aguilera, daughter of wealthy landowner Don Aguilera. Rosa lived in her chalet, but one day, Julio Leytes, in charge of the family's stable, knocked on her door and fell madly in love. Without the acceptance of the Aguilera family, they decide to flee to the neighboring province of San Juan, where they form their family having 6 children, Evaristo the first.

It is the same Evaristo who, 30 years later when he returned to Mendoza, bought his first farm, continuing the family legacy of the passion for the vine. He is united in marriage with Ada Brandi with whom they have 4 children being Julio Leytes the first of them. Inheriting from his ancestors the enthusiasm for cultivating and respecting the vineyards of Mendoza.

Today

Currently Diego and Aldana, continue this legacy, motivated by love and identity from the roots...

What is the origin of our concentrated grape juice?

Concentrated grape juice comes from natural juice squeezed directly from the grapes of Our vineyards. It is a juice that is extracted during the early stages of winemaking, and with the that the whole process begins until the final product is obtained.

What is the difference between wine and concentrated grape juice?

The grape juice concentrate is the first part after the grape press, the juice of grape itself after the grapes have been harvested. Wine, on the other hand, is the result of the alcoholic fermentation of this concentrated grape juice, in addition to others Procedures to reach the final product.

Can the facilities be visited?

Of course, both our vineyards and our winery are 100% available to be visited and traveled at all times of the year.

How do we transport our products?

Using steel drums, wooden bins, as well as large loads in flexitanks, We guarantee the safety and conservation of the properties of the grape. We offer the Possibility of sending grape juice in bulk or storing them refrigerated and/or frozen in drums, which can be transported by air, land or sea. In this way, every year we are chosen as a partner and supplier of grape juice. concentrated by those who trust and test our high standards.
